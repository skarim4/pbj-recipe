Tribute Page for Cristiano Ronaldo

Born in 1985, Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese soccer player who is currently playing for the pro league in Saudi Arabia. Joining the sport over the 20 years ago, Ronaldo has risen to the status of being one of the all time greatest. Throughout his lengthy career, he has received 33 trophies and has won many awards. After playing for 7 teams throughout his career, he is currently playing for the Saudi football club Al Nassr.

Accomplishments

  1. He has won the Ballon D'or over five times
  2. He has won the Eurpoean championship one time
  3. He is the top scorer in Fifa with a total of 923 career goals
  4. Has been nominated three times for Fifa's best men player
  5. He has won the Champion's League five times

Facts About Ronaldo

  • Ronaldo became the first person to score at 5 different world cups
  • He has worn four different shirt numbers throughout his career
  • He is the highest goalscorer internationally among active players, with 118 goals for his country
